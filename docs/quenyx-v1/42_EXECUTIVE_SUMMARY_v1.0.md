## The release

Quenyx vOPS HUB **v1.0.0** is the first General Availability release, completing the original product
roadmap. It unifies monitoring, asset management, automation, incident response, knowledge, service desk,
notifications, and compliance into **one enterprise operations platform** with a shared AI layer and a
single, evidence-based executive view.

## What customers get at v1.0.0

- **One platform, ten modules.** QynSight, QynAsset, QynRun, QynReact, QynKnow, QynSupport, QynNotify,
  QynShield, QynBalance, and QynVA — all enabled, all consistent.
- **An Enterprise AI Operator (QynVA).** Discovers what the platform can do, builds a single context
  across every module, and proposes evidence-based, cross-module action plans — never executing on its
  own, always auditable and editable.
- **Executive Intelligence.** A board-ready dashboard of operational, infrastructure, and compliance
  health, top risks, top recommendations, KPIs, and an AI executive summary — all grounded in real data.
- **Cost Intelligence (QynBalance).** Infrastructure cost, optimization, and budget forecasting from real
  platform data, with strict honesty: no invented financial figures.
- **Self-aware platform.** Built-in Platform Health and Enterprise Analytics (MTTD, MTTR, trends,
  adoption) make the platform observable and measurable.

## Why it's trustworthy

- **Evidence-based, no fabrication.** Every number traces to real rows; where data or pricing is missing,
  the platform says so rather than guessing.
- **Secure by construction.** Multi-tenant workspace isolation, UUID-only addressing, role-based access
  control, and a complete audit trail on every action.
- **Bilingual.** Full English/Arabic across all new surfaces.
- **No regressions.** v1.0.0 is additive; all prior capabilities are preserved.

## Business impact

A single pane of glass for operations leaders: faster detection and response (MTTD/MTTR visibility),
proactive optimization (cost and capacity), governed AI assistance across the whole estate, and an
executive narrative that is defensible because it is built only on real evidence.
